# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition Analysis | Power BI 💼

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, job roles, and key HR metrics.

The dashboard helps HR teams and management identify attrition patterns, workforce trends, and employee-related insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 📌 Workforce Overview
- Total Employee Count
- Employee Attrition Tracking
- Attrition Rate Analysis
- Average Employee Age
- Average Salary Analysis
- Average Years at Company

### 👥 Employee Demographics
- Gender-wise Attrition Analysis
- Age Group Analysis
- Education Background Analysis
- Workforce Distribution Insights

### 📊 Attrition Analysis
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Years at Company

### 🏢 Department Analysis
- Human Resources
- Research & Development
- Sales Department

### 📈 Interactive Reporting
- Dynamic Filters
- Department-wise Analysis
- Drill-down Insights
- Interactive Visualizations
